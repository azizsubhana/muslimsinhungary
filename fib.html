Food In Budapest

  1. Home
  2. Halal Dining
  3. Food In Budapest

As Budapest is the capital city you can find variety of cuisines ranging from Pakistani, Indian, Indonesian to Turkish to Syrian. Muslim visitors will be able to locate several Halal restaurants in Budapest. Muslim tourists must however be aware that while several restaurants claim to be Halal, they may not be as for them not serving or selling pork is “Halal”.

Hungarian cuisine is renowned for featuring ingredients like paprika and cabbage. Paprika is in fact one of the most commonly used spices in Hungarian food. Visitors to Hungary must try out iconic dishes such the popular Langos, Chimney Cake and Goulash. Most restaurants throughout the country are sure to serve variations of Goulash, but Muslim visitors may not be able to try it as it may not be Halal. Muslim visitors could try to find a place that serves a completely vegetarian or seafood version of this dish. If unable to, it is best for them to dine at the Halal certified or Muslim owned restaurants in Hungary. Chimney Cake could be considered as halal and langos if you are sure they are not frying it in the same oil where they have fried haram meat and meat products. Be aware of zsíros kenyér.

Must checkout out diction area where you can find foods to avoid.

Muslim tourists will also be able to prepare their own meals, if cooking facilities are available at their accommodation, as there are a few known halal meat shops and shops to buy other regional food items in Budapest.
