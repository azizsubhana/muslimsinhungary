The Hungarian currency is Forint or Hungarian Forint [Sign: Ft; Code: HUF].

Hungarian currency has the following denominations:



Banknotes – 500 Ft, 1,000 Ft, 2,000 Ft, 5,000 Ft, 10,000 Ft, and 20,000 Ft.


Coins – 5 Ft, 10 Ft, 20 Ft,50 Ft, 100 Ft, 200 Ft.

Money Exchange

There are many options in major cities of Hungary for exchanging cash, which includes kiosks, shops and shopping malls which sometimes offers the best rates. Most often, kiosks and money exchange shops are located at the tourist areas.

Money exchange is also available at banks at a surcharge.

In Budapest, ATMs are easy to find for exchanging from Euros to Forint.

Some hotels, restaurants, sightseeing tours often quotes the prices in Euros. There are selected supermarkets which accept Euros.

Note:

Be aware of the people who are exchanging money at the streets or tourist points.
Never exchange money from the places which did not provide receipts.
