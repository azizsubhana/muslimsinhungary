Budapest Mosque

The Budapest Mosque is easily accessible by public transport. It is a huge building with 3 floors and a parking place. The maintainer of the Budapest Mosque is the Organization of Muslims in Hungary. The gates of the Budapest Mosque were first opened in Ramadan in 2011. About 500 people pray in the Budapest Mosque on every Friday.
